Guide on using StudentSaver

This helpful website contains many options to help plan out your university application process.

- The About tab will give you information abaout our website and the aim of this application
- You can get in contact with the organizers for more help or information.
- Go to the Calendar or Deadline tab to add calender reminders by clicking the plus button (+), to add upcoming scholarship or application deadlines.
- A stress management tab is available to students who are nervous and stressed with school and university applications. You can find videos on breathing
exercises or tutorials, for stress management.

About StudentSaver

This website helps university students organize their university information.
Aim: to ensure organization among grade 12 students.

1. Students can access a calendar to add and showcase upcoming deadlines
2. Students can set goals to clearly layout what they would like to achieve on a weekly/monthly basis
3. Students can plan and rank Universities and programs
4. A stress management tab is available to students who are nervous and stressed with school and university applications
